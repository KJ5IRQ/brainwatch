## [0.1.0] - 2026-07-15

### Added

- OpenRouter discovery with exact zero-price catalog verification.
- Configurable OpenAI-compatible providers with explicit free-model allowlists.
- Structured availability probes with bounded retries and timeouts.
- Provider-qualified JSONL evidence ledgers.
- History-aware ranking and atomic JSON/text reports.
- Provider isolation and bounded concurrent probing.
- Local status, trend, provider, and chain CLI commands.
- Optional reversible OpenClaw chain integration.
- Generic user-level systemd service and timer.
- Python 3.11 through 3.13 CI, packaging checks, and security scans.
